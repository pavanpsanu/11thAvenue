Frequently Asked Questions

What is the check-in and check-out time?

Standard check-in time is 2:00 PM and check-out is at 11:00 AM. Early check-in or late check-out is subject to availability and may incur an extra charge.

Is parking available at the property?

Yes, we offer secure complimentary parking on the premises for our guests.

Are laundry services provided? Is hot water available 24/7?

Yes, we offer professional laundry services and the building is equipped with 24/7 hot water supply for all apartments.

Are appliances provided in the apartments? Is there an elevator?

Each apartment is fully equipped with modern appliances including a refrigerator, cooking stovetops, and a washing machine. The property also features a high-speed elevator for convenient access to all floors.

Are pets allowed at the serviced apartment?

No, pets are not allowed at the property to ensure a comfortable and allergy-free environment for all our guests.
